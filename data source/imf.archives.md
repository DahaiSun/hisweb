# IMF Archives Data Source

## Website
- IMF Archives: https://www.imf.org/en/about/archives
- Archives Catalog: https://archivescatalog.imf.org/search/simple

## Why This Helps
- High-credibility primary source for IMF policy and institutional history.
- Useful for major monetary and financial events tied to IMF decisions.
- Strong fit for evidence and citation quality in your timeline.

## Recommended Usage For This Project
1. Use IMF archives as authoritative references for IMF-related events.
2. Store citation fields: collection/fonds, reference code, date, URL, and notes.
3. Pair IMF sources with external market sources to explain market impact.
4. Prioritize IMF sources for events on exchange systems, debt, and stabilization programs.

## Caveats
- Not all materials are digitized or openly available online.
- Some records have access restrictions and review timelines.
- Always verify usage/copyright conditions per item.
